APARTMENT 2 TROGIR

This tastefully furnished holiday apartment is located on the 4th floor of a residential building. It is comfortable and from the balcony you have a beautiful view on the old town of Trogir and the island Čiovo. Take a walk along the coast and discover the many leisure activities, which are offered during the summer months. Near the apartment is a bus station from which you can go to other popular cities such as Split, Solin and Šibenik. Attractive beaches can also be found on the island Čiovo that is connected by a bridge to the mainland and old town of Trogir.
